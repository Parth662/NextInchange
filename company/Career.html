A BIT MORE ABOUT US
Started by designers who sweat every pixel

Phenomenon Studio was founded in 2019 as a small group of designers obsessed with grounding exquisite design in clear logic while infusing high-end aesthetics into every pixel.

Today, we’re an award-winning product design and development agency of 70+ incredibly talented people collaborating with global startups and SMEs that demand excellence in work, quality in execution, and maturity in operations – the same qualities we look for when we hire.

WHAT GUIDES US
Values we stand for

Thoughtful and open-minded

01

We listen deeply and challenge our own assumptions

This is about avoiding superficial, knee-jerk conclusions. We listen, question our own assumptions, and stand up for the objective outcome, not for ourselves or being “personally right.

Genuinely friendly

02

Built on honesty and mutual respect

Honesty and unforced mutual respect in the team. We strive to create a space where everyone feels seen and respected – a place to grow as a professional and as a person while staying true to oneself.

Responsible and accountable

03

From both the team and the company

It’s not only what we expect from employees; it’s the company’s readiness to model the same values toward employees, clients, and partners.

Growth-oriented

04

Your development is part of the plan

We recognize people as our most important asset, support their development, and constantly improve both the team and the studio’s processes.

Proactive and adaptable

05

You don’t need permission to make things better

We spot opportunities and propose solutions, even for small, local fixes. Tactical improvements open the door to strategic change.

Client-focused

06

We solve problems that matter

Helping create tangible value for a client’s business is our primary way of driving the company’s growth and each person’s opportunities

Knowledgeable and collaborative

07

Deep expertise shared across the team

We’re oriented toward end results, and active knowledge-sharing matters to us because we aim for every specialist to develop deep expertise in their field.

Extra benefits
Remote work 
with flexible hours

Work from anywhere and set your own hours. Just stay connected with the team and get the work done. Freedom with responsibility.

Autonomy and ownership
in your projects

Annual performance reviews with salary revisions

We review your work annually. When you grow and deliver, your salary reflects that. Your growth, your reward. It’s that simple.

Workshops and learning
playgrounds
